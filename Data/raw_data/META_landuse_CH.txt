Areal_NOAS92_prop1km.grd is a raster stack containing the proportional land use at 1km resolution in years 1992-1997. Land use information stems from the Swiss land use statistics (see pdf for explanation). The raster layers give percentage cover for the different categories, for basic categories and for aggregated land use classes. The legend for these can be found in the Arealstatistik1992.pdf. The columns "GESAMT" show how much area of that cell were covered in the data - some cells near the Swiss border are truncated.

Areal_NOAS04_prop1km.grd is a raster stack for the proportional land use at 1km resolution in years 2004-2009. Legend can be found in Arealstatistik_2004.pdf. The columns "GESAMT" show how much area of that cell were covered in the data - some cells near the Swiss border are truncated.
